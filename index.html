Six Proven Sales Strategies for Growth

1

Ideal Customer Profile Development

30-Day High Impact

Define 2-3 specific customer personas based on demographics, project size, and wellness priorities. Research shows salespeople using detailed customer profiles are 230% more productive.

Key Actions:

  • Target tech professionals ($75K+ projects)
  • Focus on wellness-conscious families ($25K-75K)
  • Develop luxury homeowner segment ($100K+)
  • Create wellness-focused discovery questions
Expected ROI: 25% improvement in lead-to-appointment conversion, $1,350 average increase in project values.
2

Value-Based Pricing Strategy

30-Day High Impact

Position projects as wellness investments rather than renovations. Premium contractors using value-based pricing report 25% higher profit margins.

Key Actions:

  • Create "Wellness Investment Calculator"
  • Develop three-tier service packages
  • Emphasize health benefits in materials
  • Leverage NARI awards for premium positioning
Expected ROI: 10-25% price premiums, target margins of 30-35% for mid-range, 35-40% for large projects.
3

Digital Pre-Sales Research

30-Day High Impact

Prepare customized presentations based on prospect research before meetings. Companies adapting to this approach close deals faster and at higher rates.

Key Actions:

  • Research prospects via Google Maps and tax records
  • Create property-specific project examples
  • Develop tablet-based neighborhood presentations
  • Prepare wellness talking points by lifestyle
Expected ROI: Sales cycle reduction from 6-8 weeks to 4-6 weeks, 20% improvement in first-appointment close rates.
4

Systematic Referral Program

60-Day Medium Impact

Referral customers have 4x higher purchase likelihood and cost 5-10x less to acquire. Simply Home's 4.8/5 rating provides strong foundation.

Key Actions:

  • Implement dual-sided referral rewards (1% of contract)
  • Optimize real estate partnerships (2-3% commission)
  • Create 30/90/365-day customer touch points
  • Partner with wellness professionals
Expected ROI: 50% increase in referral volume, referral leads convert at 25-40% vs. 2-5% for cold leads.
5

CRM Implementation & Automation

60-Day Medium Impact

Construction-specific CRMs deliver significant ROI. JobNimbus reports 43% average revenue increases for mid-sized remodeling companies.

Key Actions:

  • Deploy BuilderTrend or JobNimbus platform
  • Automate lead nurturing sequences
  • Implement mobile field capabilities
  • Create follow-up automation workflows
Expected ROI: Save 8-10 hours per person per week, 15-25% improvement in lead conversion, $200K-500K revenue potential.
6

Educational Content Marketing

60-Day Medium Impact

Position Simply Home as the wellness expert through valuable educational content. This approach aligns with women-owned, transparent business models.

Key Actions:

  • Create wellness-focused blog content
  • Develop "Healthy Home Assessment" lead magnet
  • Produce wellness transformation case studies
  • Launch educational email campaigns
Expected ROI: 617% growth in marketing-qualified leads, 30% increase in average project value through enhanced positioning.
